We are one of the largest, most diverse universities with over 900 students in Bangladesh.

The Z. H. Sikder University of Science and Technology a coeducational privately owned university is dedicated to advancing knowledge and educating students in science, technology, and other areas of scholarship that will best serve the nation and the world in the 21st century. The University has been established in 2012 and planned to be organized into four faculties - Humanities and Social Sciences, Life Sciences, Business Studies, and Engineering & Technology. This University is situated at Madhupur Village of Bhadergonj Upazila of Shariatpur district in Southern Bangladesh...
